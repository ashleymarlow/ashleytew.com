A little about me

Ashley is an award winning multidisciplinary designer and illustrator living in the Pacific Northwest. She holds over 5 years of professional experience working in agency environments and with freelance clients. Her work has been featured in HGTV Magazine and the Gonzaga University Magazine. Some of her past clients include Gonzaga University, American Advertising Agency Spokane, Hoopfest, Spokane AAU, Boys & Girls Club, and Global Credit Union.
